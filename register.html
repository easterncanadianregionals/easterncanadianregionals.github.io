Registration

Registration is required for all contestants entering Championship and Junior divisions. Spectators may attend the event for free.

Please note the deadline for regular registration AND music upload is Sunday May 11. After that, a late fee will apply.

Step 1: Registration fee

Please review the contest's format carefully, and select the division(s) you'd like to enter. Then click the "Register" button which will bring you to the payment page.

  • You can have someone else (parent, friend, etc) pay for you. In step 2 below, you will enter the actual contestant's information.
  • Payment is processed by PayPal, but you can pay as a guest using a credit card without a PayPal account.
  • If this does not work, please contact the organizer for alternatives.
  Championship 1A$25
  Championship Open   $25
  Junior$15
Total (CAD)$0

Step 2: Music upload

In this step you will enter your contestant information and upload the music for your routine(s).

You can upload or change your music as many time as you like before Sunday May 11. After that, a late fee will apply.
