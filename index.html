About Eastern Canadian Regionals

Welcome to the official website of the Eastern Canadian Regionals 2025! Some of the finest return top players from Eastern Canada will be competing for this year's regional titles in Toronto on Sunday May 18. As sanctioned by the Canadian Yo-yo Association (CANYA), the top 3 contestants from Ontario and Quebec will be seeded to the Canadian National Return Top Championship 2025. Participants from other provinces or countries are also welcome to compete for prizes.

This event is family-friendly and open to the public for free. Spectators may enter this event without admission fees. Contestants entering Championship or Junior divisions must register here.

The social media hashtag is #ECR2025. To receive the latest updates or get in touch with us, make sure to follow CANYA on social media.
