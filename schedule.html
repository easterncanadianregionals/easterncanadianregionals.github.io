Schedule

Eastern Canadian Regionals is a one-day event starting at 10am and ending at 4:30pm. The event is open to the public and FREE to attend for spectators, so bring your friends and family!!

See the results page for the order of freestyle performances.

Sport ladder event will take place on stage at 2pm. Anyone is welcome to enter on the spot.

10:00Doors Open
10:30Championship 1A division - Preliminaries
11:30Junior division
12:00Lunch
13:30Championship Open division
14:00Sport Ladder
14:30Championship 1A division - Finals
16:00Results and awards
